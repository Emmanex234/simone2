About Simone Susinna

Simone Susinna is an Italian actor and model, born on November 14, 1993, in Catania, Italy. He has made a significant impact in both the fashion and entertainment industries.

Career Highlights

  • Featured in major fashion campaigns for Dolce & Gabbana
  • Breakout role in Netflix's "365 Days: This Day" (2022)
